What Harlo does

Everything you need to show up for yourself every day

😌
Daily mood check-in
A gentle nudge each day to pause and notice how you're actually feeling. No pressure, no judgement — just you and your feelings.
✍️
Guided journaling
Thoughtful daily prompts that help you go deeper. Write as little or as much as you want — Harlo meets you where you are.
💬
AI reflection
After you journal, Harlo's AI responds with warmth and curiosity — not advice, just genuine reflection that helps you feel heard.
📈
Mood tracking
See your mood patterns across the week. Understanding your emotional rhythm is one of the most powerful things you can do for your wellbeing.
🫂
Grounding tools
Breathing exercises, grounding techniques, and crisis helplines — all in one place for when things get hard.
👥
Supportive community
Share anonymously, cheer others on, and feel less alone. A safe, moderated space built for people who get it.
