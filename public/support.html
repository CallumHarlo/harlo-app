Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot password?" on the sign in screen and we'll send a reset link to your email.

Is my data private?
Yes. Your journal entries are private and only visible to you. We never share your personal data.

How do I delete my account?
Email us at support@harlo-app.com and we'll delete your account and all data within 48 hours.

How do I report a user?
Tap the three dots on any community post and select "Report". We review all reports within 24 hours.

Community Guidelines

Harlo is a safe space for mental wellness. We have zero tolerance for abusive, offensive or harmful content. Users who violate our guidelines will be removed.
